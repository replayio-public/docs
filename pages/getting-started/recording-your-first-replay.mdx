# Recording your first replay

There are just few simple steps to create your first recording:

<Steps>
### Download the Replay browser
<Cards>
<Card icon={<IcBaselineApple />} title="Mac" href="https://www.replay.io/downloads/replay.dmg" />
<Card icon={<CodiconTerminalLinux />} title="Linux" href="https://www.replay.io/downloads/linux-replay.tar.bz2" />
</Cards>
[Or follow these instructions](/getting-started/replay-browser/installation#cli-replay-chrome-new) on how to install use our CLI tool.

<Video src={downloadReplayMwnjr} />

### Install Replay on your computer

Follow the standard installation steps or check out our docs to get a more detailed information.

### Open Replay and click the <RecButton className="w-12 mb-0.5 inline-block" /> button 

You can record a replay in the same way that you record a video. Navigate to the page you want to record, click the <RecButton className="w-12 mb-0.5 inline-block" /> button, 
capture the issue, and click the <StopButton className="w-14 mb-0.5 inline-block" /> button to save the replay.
