Replay teams are a shared space to organize replays and collaborate across your team.

## Setting up a team

To create a team:

1. Navigate to the Replay Library.
2. Click "Create Team" in the sidebar.
3. Give the team a name.
4. Invite users by email address or invite link.

<Callout type="info" emoji="ðŸ‘‰">
ðŸ’¡ Sharing the invite link is the easiest way to invite everyone on your team. New members added by invite link are Developers by default.

## Adding a replay to your team

Replays can be added to the team by selecting the team in the replay upload screen right after stopping the recording.

![Screen Shot 2021-09-23 at 3.18.31 PM.png](/images/Screen_Shot_2021-09-23_at_3.18.31_PM_nqrrs.png)

## API Keys

Teams can create API Keys for uploading [source maps](/getting-started/teams-admin/uploading-source-maps) during deploys or uploading replays recorded programmatically via our [Node](/reference-guide/recording/replay-node), [Playwright](/test-suites/playwright/installation), or **[Puppeteer](/test-suites/puppeteer)** recorders.
