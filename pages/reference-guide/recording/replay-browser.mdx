# Replay Browser

The Replay Browser helps you record your web application and debug it later with time travel enabled browser devtools. 

* You can use Replay as your development browser to record and debug difficult issues 
* You can use Replay to record bug reports and share the replays with your team 
* You can use Replay to record your E2E Tests and debug failures later.

![Replay Browser](/images/replay-browser-overboard.png)

## Runtime / Platform support


When you download the browser today you get our Firefox based browser, but we'll be switching over to [Chrome](/getting-started/replay-browser/install-chrome) soon and if you're open to launching Chrome from the CLI you can do that today.
